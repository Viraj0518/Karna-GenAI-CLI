## Facility Requirements & Logistics

### Space Requirements (per site)
- **Primary Room:** 400–500 sq ft, AV-equipped, round table seating for 10
- **Check-in Area:** Registration, consent, honorarium distribution
- **Breakout Room:** Observation/technical setup
- **Accessibility:** ADA compliance, parking, transit access, signage

### Technology Stack
- Video conferencing: Zoom with recording + moderation backup
- Participant database: REDCap with real-time status tracking
- Recordings: Encrypted local storage + cloud backup
- Transcription: Human-verified transcript QA within 48 hours

### Honorariums & Logistics
- **Per-Participant:** $75 gift card (venue-specific options)
- **Moderator Incentive:** $200/session
- **Total 30 Groups Cost:** ~$22,500 (participant + facilitator)

## Risk Register & Mitigation

| # | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|------|------------|--------|-----------|
| **1** | **Low recruitment conversion (<70% of targets)** | Medium | High | • Pre-recruitment engagement with community orgs; increase screening pool by 25%; offer incentive boosts in weeks 3–4 if needed; rolling recruitment windows per city |
| **2** | **Facility cancellations/unavailability** | Low | High | • Secure 2 backup venues per city now; contingency budget ($3K); virtual pivot plan for 2–3 groups if on-site unavailable; 2-week facility confirmations |
| **3** | **Moderator illness/absence during groups** | Medium | Medium | • On-call reserve moderator (Dr. James Liu) pre-briefed; backup scripts & protocols; cross-train 1 admin per city; daily moderator health check-ins starting week 3 |
| **4** | **Recording/data quality failures (AV, transcript issues)** | Low | High | • Dual recording setup (primary + backup); test all A/V day-before each session; live transcription monitoring; manual transcript validation QA; encrypted storage redundancy |
| **5** | **Participant no-shows (>15% per session)** | Medium | Medium | • SMS reminders 48h + 24h before groups; confirmation calls 72h before; overbooking by 20%; honorarium pre-distribution to incentivize attendance; rapid replacement recruitment list per city on standby |
