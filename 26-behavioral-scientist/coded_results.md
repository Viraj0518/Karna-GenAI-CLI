## Key Behavioral Patterns

1. **Trust Mediation** (A1, P3): Provider trust and peer social proof are strongest predictors of vaccine uptake
2. **Information Quality Over Quantity** (K2, K3): Conflicting sources create paralysis; need curated, authoritative guidance
3. **Emotional Primacy** (A3, A2): Risk perceptions driven by anecdotes and emotional resonance, not data
4. **Deliberation Value** (P2): Rushed decisions resisted; offering time increases acceptance
5. **Community Norms** (P3): Family and peer behavior influence decisions more than mass messaging
