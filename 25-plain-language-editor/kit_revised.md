# COVID-19 Booster Messaging Kit

## Background

New COVID-19 variants are spreading quickly and can make previous vaccines less effective. Booster shots help protect against serious illness and death, especially for older people and those with weakened immune systems.

## What You Should Know About Boosters

Your doctor can help you decide if a booster shot is right for you. Studies show that booster shots strengthen your body's protection against COVID-19 and make vaccines work better. Talk to your healthcare provider about the benefits and any side effects from booster shots.

## How to Get a Booster Shot

We're making boosters easy to get. Here's what you need to do:
- Find an appointment near you online or by calling your local health center
- Learn more from fact sheets and videos we provide
- Ask community health workers at your clinic if you have questions
