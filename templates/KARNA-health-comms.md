# Karna LLC — Health Communications Team

## Company

Karna LLC is a public health consulting firm. Top-10 CDC contractor. NAICS: 541611, 541690, 541910. HIPAA-bound.

## Team

Health Communications & Social Marketing — campaign development, message testing, rapid community assessments, data storytelling, digital media for CDC and federal health agencies.

## Tech Stack

- Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop)
- Canva (quick social assets)
- Microsoft Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel)
- Qualtrics (survey/message testing)
- Social media management tools
- Git (for content version control)

## Key Work Types

- Communication campaign strategies (purpose statement through analysis)
- Multi-channel content (print, digital, social media, dating app ads, radio)
- Rapid Community Assessment (RCA) guides and reports
- Focus group discussion guides and analysis
- Data storytelling (quantitative data + narrative + visuals)
- Provider/patient educational materials

## Audiences

- General public (health campaigns)
- Black and Latinx communities (vaccine confidence)
- Young adults, rural residents, parents/caregivers
- Healthcare providers (OB-GYNs, primary care)
- State/local health department staff
- Community-based organizations

## Conventions

- All public-facing content must be at 8th-grade reading level or below
- Use plain language guidelines (plainlanguage.gov)
- All materials need 508 accessibility compliance
- Content must be culturally appropriate and tested with target audiences
- Cite sources using CDC/APA style

## Rules for Nellie

- Never generate health claims without evidence citations
- Always flag content that could be misinterpreted as medical advice
- Adapt reading level to target audience (specify in prompt)
- Include alt-text recommendations for all image descriptions
- Follow CDC's health equity communication principles
