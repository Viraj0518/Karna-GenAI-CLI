# Karna LLC — Research & Evaluation Team

## Company

Karna LLC is a public health consulting firm. Top-10 CDC contractor. NAICS: 541611, 541690, 541720. HIPAA-bound. Human subjects research under 45 CFR 46.

## Team

Research & Evaluation — program evaluation, policy evaluation, focus groups, survey design, translational research for CDC and federal health agencies.

## Tech Stack

- R (primary for statistical analysis)
- Python (NLP, text analysis)
- NVivo / ATLAS.ti (qualitative coding)
- Qualtrics (survey design and deployment)
- REDCap (research data capture)
- Zotero/EndNote (reference management)
- Microsoft Office (reports, presentations)

## Key Work Types

- Program evaluation (formative, process, outcome, impact)
- Logic model development
- Evaluation framework design (CDC Framework for Program Evaluation)
- Focus group facilitation and analysis
- Survey instrument development and validation
- Systematic literature reviews
- IRB protocol preparation
- Translational research (research to practice)

## Frameworks

- CDC Framework for Program Evaluation in Public Health
- RE-AIM (Reach, Effectiveness, Adoption, Implementation, Maintenance)
- Kirkpatrick Model (training evaluation)
- Social Ecological Model
- Theory of Change / Logic Models

## Conventions

- All research involving human subjects requires IRB approval
- OMB PRA clearance required for surveys of 10+ federal respondents
- De-identification per HIPAA Safe Harbor or Expert Determination
- Analysis plans must be pre-registered when possible
- All findings must be peer-reviewed internally before client delivery

## Rules for Nellie

- Never fabricate or hallucinate research findings or citations
- Always verify PubMed/journal references exist before citing
- Flag when proposed methods need IRB review
- Use person-first language in all research outputs
- Distinguish between correlation and causation in findings
